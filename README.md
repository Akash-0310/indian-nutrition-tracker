# ThaliTrack — Calorie & Nutrition Tracker for Indian Food

The calorie tracker that actually knows what a dal makhani is.

## What it is

ThaliTrack is a nutrition tracker built India-first, for people whose daily plates look like dal, sabzi, roti, biryani, dosa, and chaat — not "chicken breast, 100g." It replaces the frustrating ritual of hunting through global nutrition databases for your actual food with a tracker that speaks the same kitchen language you do.

Users search a curated library of real Indian dishes, log what they ate, pick a serving size, and instantly see a live calorie ring and macro breakdown for the day. No onboarding quiz, no weight logging, no guilt-tripping streak mechanics.

## Who it's for

- **Fitness-conscious professionals** in India and the diaspora who want to manage their intake without translating every meal.
- **Runners, lifters, and athletes** balancing performance with rotis and dal.
- **Families and meal preppers** planning a week of breakfasts, lunches, and dinners.
- **People managing diabetes, PCOS, or weight goals** who need honest, India-relevant numbers.
- **Nutritionists and coaches** who want to share simple tracking tools with their clients.

## Use cases

- Log today's breakfast (2 dosas + chutney) in under 15 seconds.
- Track the full day's calories and macros with a live ring and color-coded bar.
- Jump between days to backfill yesterday's lunch or plan tomorrow's thali.
- Set your own daily calorie target (800–5000 kcal) with no "recommended" nags.
- Browse a searchable catalog of 80+ dishes across breads, rice, dal, sabzi, South Indian, snacks, sweets, and drinks.
- Adjust serving sizes (0.5× to 10×) to match real portions, not lab portions.
- Store everything on your device — no login, no data leaving your browser.

## Core capabilities

- **80+ curated Indian dishes** with realistic home-kitchen calorie and macro values.
- **11 food categories** covering breads, rice, dal, sabzi, non-veg, South Indian, snacks, breakfast, sweets, and drinks.
- **Live daily calorie ring + macro bar** that updates as you log.
- **Breakfast / Lunch / Snacks / Dinner slots** so the day reads like a real day.
- **Date navigation** for backfilling and planning.
- **Local-first storage** for full offline use and full privacy.
- **Light and dark mode** plus smooth animations, glassmorphism UI, and full responsiveness.
- **Companion pages** — Home, About, FAQ, Resources, Contact — so the whole site feels complete and credible.

## The idea in one line

ThaliTrack is the nutrition tracker built for the way India actually eats — calm, honest, private, and delightful to use every single day.
